Margeritta

Usually this pizza is made with basil, mozzarella cheese and tomatoes in imitation of the colors of the italian flag.

Marinara

This is traditional Neapolitan pizza that is made with lots of garlic, anchovies and oregano.

Capricciosa

This pizza is topped with olives, artichoke hearts, half of a boiled egg, prosciutto and mushrooms.

Sfincione

This is a thick sheet pizza that is made in Sicily and uses anchovies, caciocavallo, breadcrumbs and tomato sauce.

Quattro formaggi

This pizza is made using a four cheese combination of mozarella, parmiiano-reggiano, ricota and Gorgonzola.

Romana

This is a pizza that contains anchovies, capperi, mozarella and tomatoes.
